Personal Profile

I'm a mechatronic engineer from Metropolitan technological Institute (ITM) (Medellin, Colombia). I received my bachelor degree in 2016. My undergraduate research was developed in fields such as numerical methods, finite elements, mechatronics, robotics, computer vision and artificial intelligence. In 2014, I received the research merit award from Faculty of Engineering, ITM. In 2015 I was "young researcher" at the same institution, Developing a research in multi-focus image processing. Currently, I'm researching in Haptics and Human-Computer Interaction at Computer Graphics Group in the Informatics institute from the UFRGS in Porto Alegre, Brazil. I have interest in Haptics-Related areas such as psychophysics and tactile perception.
